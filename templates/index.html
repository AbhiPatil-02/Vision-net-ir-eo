EO/IR Image Classifications

📌 About the Project

This project enables rapid classification of Electro-Optical (EO) and Infrared (IR) imagery for military and surveillance use. Leveraging pre-trained object detection models, the system accurately identifies key targets such as personnel, vehicles, armored units, aircraft, and weapons — supporting real-time situational awareness in challenging environments.

🎯 Objective

To support critical decision-making in defense environments by improving the interpretation of EO/IR imagery, particularly under adverse conditions such as:

  • 🌙 Low light or night operations
  • 🌲 Camouflaged terrains
  • 🌫️ Visual obstructions like fog,
    smoke, or haze
